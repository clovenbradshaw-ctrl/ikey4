About & Privacy

iKey runs entirely in your browser. Favorites and recent locations are saved in localStorage on your device.

Once loaded, the app works offline and no data leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

You can set a password to encrypt your saved data so only you can read it.

To clear your data, open your browser settings and remove site data for this page or use the "Clear browsing data" option.
