About iKey

Version 1.0
iKey helps you keep your location secure while providing quick access to emergency services. Your privacy and safety are our top priorities.

iKey is not affiliated with Proton or any other services. Proton is referenced solely for privacy and convenience. No usage data is stored; users are responsible for securely backing up their data and for using strong passwords and two-factor authentication where necessary.
