🔒 Uncompromising Security Features

🛡️ Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Your data is encrypted on your device before upload. Even Filen cannot access your files - only you have the keys.

🔐 Military-Grade Encryption

AES-256 encryption standard used by governments and military organizations worldwide.

🌍 GDPR Compliant

Based in Germany with strict European privacy laws. Your data is protected by some of the world's strongest privacy regulations.

🔑 Client-Side Encryption

All encryption happens on your device. Your password never leaves your computer unencrypted.

✨ Key Features for Emergency Preparedness

  • 📁 Secure Document Storage: Store medical records, insurance cards, IDs, and prescriptions
  • 💬 Encrypted Chat: Communicate securely with family members during emergencies
  • 📱 Cross-Platform Access: Available on Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, and Android
  • 🔄 Automatic Sync: Your files stay updated across all your devices
  • 📤 Secure Sharing: Share emergency documents with healthcare providers using encrypted links
  • 🗂️ Version History: Recover previous versions of important documents
  • 🖼️ Photo Backup: Automatically backup photos of important documents from your phone
  • ⚡ Fast Performance: Quick upload and download speeds for emergency access

💡 Emergency Preparedness Tips

Create a dedicated "Emergency" folder in Filen with:

  • Copies of all family members' IDs and passports
  • Insurance policies and cards
  • Medical records and prescription lists
  • Emergency contact list with phone numbers
  • Photos of valuable items for insurance claims
  • Important legal documents (will, power of attorney)
